## Overview

🍃 ~ Psithurism is a mod centered around Japanese culture! Create onsen baths, build japanese houses and temples, cover your floor in tatami, and sit down to enjoy the culture of the land of the rising sun!

----------------

⛩️ ~ **Featuring:**

- New "Dark Cherry" wood type and "Ashino" stone type
- Shoji (paper walls)
- Masks, school uniforms, flower crowns, fox tail & ears...
- Onsen blocks, koi fish
- Maneki-neko, functional teru teru bozū, pottery sherds
- Rice, sushi, yakitori, mochi, fugu, tofu, miso, matcha...
- Bonsai trees
- Rice & soja plants
  & much more!
